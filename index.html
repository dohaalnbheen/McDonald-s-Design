PlayGrounds

McDonald's playgrounds are called McDonald's Play Place. Some McDonald's restaurants in suburbs and some cities feature large indoor or outdoor playgrounds. The first PlayPlace with the familiar crawl tube design with ball pits and slides was introduced in 1987 in the United States, with more being built soon after.
